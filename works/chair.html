Description
An ongoing research in body movement, prosthetics and the future of chair design. The project started during the first lockdown from the observation of my personal posture and body movements while working from home. This fascination developed into tracking my body positions and looking into their relationship with my levels of creativity and productivity. Based on my findings the research developed into a speculative study on interactive chairs, formed around the statement that furniture should support each individual based on the personal demand and increase their cognitive function.
