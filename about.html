/About/
Polina Slavova // she/her // b. 1999, Bulgaria based in Arnhem / Eindhoven, Netherlands is a multidisciplinary research-based designer. The main focus of her work is artistic research on socio-political topics, social structures and behaviourism in the context of the contemporary digital and consumerist culture.

Co-founder of DuctTape Collective.
